October 2011. I was

when a

changed
everything. Over the next year my life spiraled
down to
the
depths where
I questioned my
existence, my worth and who I
was.

Double exposure experimentation. As I work with
images combining portraits with
nature
or buildings, I
am struck by a sense that
our
lives are one long
double
exposure. We are
walking dichotomies saying
one thing and doing another.
Thinking one thing but
saying
something different. There's a disharmony to
that
type of existence. A life disconnected
and
separate. Perhaps we should
be more
focused on
cultivating that single exposure life
that gives us
peace inside and out.
Perhaps.
