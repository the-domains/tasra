Audacious

"You make time for what you want.
If you're not


prioritizing the things

you care
about, consider the



possibility that you don't actually
care
about those


things." I
started

Must by Elle Luna. Halfway

down their
wildest craziest

notecards and

roastery

downtown
Seattle my husband

We

Starbucks map and

notecards. Then spent the

an hour

Dreaming. And writing down our
most


audacious, terrifying

wildest and


most audacious

hear
it!!

Double exposure




experimentation.

As I work with images combining portraits with
nature
or buildings, I
am
struck by
a sense that

our lives are
one long double exposure. We
are

walking
dichotomies
saying one thing and

doing another.
Thinking
one
thing but saying
something
different.
There's a disharmony to
that

type
of existence. A life
disconnected and

separate. Perhaps we
should be

more focused
on
cultivating that single exposure
life
that

gives us
peace inside and out. Perhaps.
